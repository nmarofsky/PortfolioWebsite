About Nick

Short timeline of my life.

  • 1997-2015

    Childhood in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

    Born in Baltimore, Maryland, I moved to Pittsburgh at the young age of 3. Throughout my childhood I enjoyed playing sports, skiing, camping, and video editing.

  • June 2015

    Awarded Eagle Scout Honor

    After being apart of the scouting family for 14 years, I had finally reached the top with the rank of Eagle Scout. An award I will cherish and be humbled by for my entire life.

  • May 2019

    Graduated with Bachelor's of Science

    I attended Indiana University of Pennsylvania's four year Communications Media program. It taught me so much about industry trends and how much has changed in the world of media over the past decades. There I graduated with a 3.4 GPA and an extremely hands on internship.

  • August 2019

    Management Trainee with Enterprise Rent-A-Car

    Just a few months after earning my degree, I landed a job with the number one rental car company in America. There I learned the day to day tasks required to run a business. Also, I got hands on experience with how to deal with customers and hit sales quotas.

  • March 2020

    Relocation to Charlotte, North Carolina

    After being in Pittsburgh for the greater part of my life, I decided to take a leap of faith and adventure down south. A couple weeks after the big move, a national pandemic broke out. This resulted in me being furloughed from enterprise and opening my eyes to a more excited career. Over the past few months, I have been deeply indulged in the world of coding. This has helped me discover not only a new career path, but a new passion.

  • May-October 2020

    NuCamp's Full-Stack Web Developer Bootcamp.

    While taking this boot camp, I acquired skills to become a full stack programmer. Full Stack Developers are computer programmers who are proficient in both front and back end coding. Their primary responsibilities include designing user interactions on websites, developing servers and databases for website functionality and coding for mobile platforms.

  • Present Day

    Professional Freelancer

    After aquiring the neccessary skills during my bootcamp, I started to recieve freelance contracts for corporations and aspiring start up companies. For these companies I have designed landing pages to gain interest from outside investors. Also, I have worked on group message software, as well as designing a mobile application that assists doctors in tracking their patient's vital records.
